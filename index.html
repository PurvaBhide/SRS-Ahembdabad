Women Entrepreneurship

We believe in unlocking the potential of women through skill development, education, and entrepreneurship. AMC’s CSR initiatives aim to uplift women and create sustainable livelihoods, fostering inclusive growth across communities.

Skill Development

Through focused skill development programs, AMC empowers individuals to gain meaningful employment, start their own ventures, and contribute to economic growth. We are committed to creating a skilled and self-reliant workforce for tomorrow.

About Us

To catalyze sustainable growth by creating an enabling environment for the corporate sector to work in partnership with the Government, Non-Government, and Civil Society Organizations, as well as Community Organizations in the field of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) has now assumed a significant importance.

The Government of Gujarat attaches due importance to the welfare activities that will be conducted for the well-being of people of the State of Gujarat. In this regard, a need has been felt for optimal utilization of the CSR (Fund, through facilitating both the Public and Private Sector Companies in relation to carrying out their CSR activities.

About Us

The Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation (AMC) is the governing civic body of Ahmedabad, Gujarat’s largest city. Established in 1950 under the Bombay Provincial Corporation Act, AMC is responsible for the administration and development of the city, ensuring essential services and infrastructure for over 8 million citizens.

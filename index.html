About Us

Welcome to Amdavad Municipal Corporation’s CSR Connect Portal , a one-stop gateway where purpose-driven funding meets on-ground impact. In today’s world, CSR isn’t just about ticking a compliance box it is about creating real, visible change. At AMC, we believe that every rupee spent on Corporate Social Responsibility has the power to transform lives, uplift communities, and build a city that thrives together.

But how do we make that happen?
This platform is our answer. A digital bridge built for corporates who want their CSR funds to matter, and government departments who know where that support is needed most. Through this portal, Amdavad Municipal Corporation brings together a curated ecosystem of ready-to-implement, high-impact projects from across departments in areas like health, education, skill development, sanitation, environment, heritage, and more. These are not abstract ideas; they are ground-level initiatives that need the right partners to bring them to life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the AMC CSR Portal?
The AMC CSR Portal is an initiative by the Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation to facilitate Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) projects in partnership with businesses, NGOs, and individuals for the city's development.
Who can contribute through this portal?
  • Companies (fulfilling CSR obligations under the Companies Act, 2013)
  • NGOs & Trusts (registered and compliant with CSR norms)
  • Individuals & Philanthropists (willing to donate to civic projects)
What kind of projects are covered under AMC's CSR?
  • Social Welfare (Women Empowerment, Child Welfare, Elderly Care)
  • Education & Skill Development
  • Healthcare & Sanitation
  • Urban Infrastructure (Parks, Roads, Water Supply)
  • Environment & Sustainability Projects
How can a company/NGO partner with AMC for CSR?
  1. Register on the AMC CSR Portal
  2. Submit a proposal for a project
  3. Get approval from AMC and implement the project
How does AMC ensure transparency in CSR fund usage?
  • All projects are monitored by AMC officials
  • Regular progress reports & audits are published
  • Donors receive tax exemptions (80G/CSR compliance certificates)
Are CSR contributions tax-deductible?
  • For Companies: Eligible under Section 135 of Companies Act
  • For Individuals/NGOs: Tax benefits under Section 80G of IT Act

About Us

The Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation (AMC) is the governing civic body of Ahmedabad, Gujarat’s largest city. Established in 1950 under the Bombay Provincial Corporation Act, AMC is responsible for the administration and development of the city, ensuring essential services and infrastructure for over 8 million citizens.

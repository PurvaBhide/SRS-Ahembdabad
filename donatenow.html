Clean Water for the Urban Area

Access to clean water is a fundamental human right, yet thousands of families in urban slums still struggle with contaminated water sources. This project aims to install water purification systems and provide safe drinking water to underserved communities in Mumbai's urban areas.

Our initiative will establish 10 community water purification centers, each capable of serving 500 families daily. These centers will use advanced filtration technology to remove harmful bacteria, viruses, and chemical contaminants, ensuring every drop of water meets WHO safety standards.

Project Details

This comprehensive water purification project will be implemented in three phases over 6 months:

  • Phase 1: Site preparation and infrastructure development
  • Phase 2: Installation of purification equipment and testing
  • Phase 3: Community training and project handover

Each water center will be equipped with multi-stage filtration systems, UV sterilization, and regular quality monitoring to ensure consistent water quality.
